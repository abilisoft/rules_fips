# Code of conduct

We want technical discussion in `rules_fips` to be rigorous, direct, and safe
for everyone participating.

## Expected behavior

- Critique claims and code with specific evidence.
- State uncertainty, especially around security and compliance boundaries.
- Respect different experience levels, identities, and viewpoints.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Accept correction and help turn it into a durable test or document.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, doxxing, sustained
personal attacks, and deliberate disruption are not acceptable in project
spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `oss@abilisoft.com`. Maintainers may edit
or remove contributions, limit participation, or ban participants when needed
to protect the community. Reports will be handled as confidentially as
practical, with conflicts of interest avoided where possible.
